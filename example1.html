Use of force type breakdown by subject's reported race.

Level 1: Force that causes transitory pain or the complaint of transitory pain.

Level 2: Force that causes or is reasonably expected to cause physical injury greater than transitory pain but less than great or substantial bodily harm.

Level 3: Force that causes or is reasonably expected to cause, great bodily harm, substantial bodily harm, loss of consciousness, or death.

Level 3 - OIS: Officer Invloved Shooting.
